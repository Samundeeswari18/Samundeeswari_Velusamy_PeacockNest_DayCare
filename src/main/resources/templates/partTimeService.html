Part-time Care

At Peacock Nest Daycare Center, we understand that flexibility is key for working parents. Our Part-time Care service is designed to provide quality care for your child while you manage your busy schedule.

Key Features:

  • Flexible Hours: Choose the days and hours that best fit your needs.
  • Qualified Staff: Experienced caregivers dedicated to providing a nurturing environment.
  • Enriching Activities: Age-appropriate activities to foster learning and development.

Daily Schedule:

  • Morning Drop-off: 8:00 AM to 9:00 AM
  • Educational Activities: 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM
  • Lunch & Nap Time: 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM
  • Afternoon Playtime: 1:30 PM to 3:00 PM
  • Pick-up Time: 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM

Contact Us: For more information or to schedule a visit, please contact us at contact@peacockdaycare.com.
