After-school Program

Our After-school Program is designed for children who need care after regular school hours. With engaging activities and a supportive environment, your child will continue to learn and grow even after school.

Key Features:

  • Homework Assistance: Help with homework and academic enrichment.
  • Creative Activities: Art, music, and physical activities to keep children engaged.
  • Healthy Snacks: Nutritious snacks provided daily.

Daily Schedule:

  • School Pickup: 3:00 PM
  • Homework Time: 3:15 PM to 4:00 PM
  • Creative Activities: 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM
  • Snack Time: 5:00 PM to 5:30 PM
  • Free Play: 5:30 PM to 6:00 PM
  • Pick-up Time: 6:00 PM

Contact Us: For more information or to enroll, please contact us at contact@peacockdaycare.com.
