Full-time Care

Our Full-time Care program offers a comprehensive solution for families needing full-day care. With a structured schedule and dedicated staff, your child will thrive in a safe and stimulating environment.

Key Features:

  • Full-Day Coverage: From 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
  • Nutritious Meals: Breakfast, lunch, and snacks provided.
  • Developmental Activities: Curriculum-based activities to support growth and learning.

Sample Daily Schedule:

  • Morning Drop-off: 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM
  • Morning Activities: 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM
  • Lunch & Nap Time: 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM
  • Afternoon Learning: 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM
  • Free Play & Pickup: 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM
